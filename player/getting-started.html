Getting Started

Private Frontier is being built as a persistent Western survival RPG. Character actions, preparation, equipment, and reputation are intended to matter.

Current Foundations

Characters

Creation, appearance saving, barber changes, and location persistence are working.

Horses

Stable ownership, equipment, and recall are working. Recovery timing still needs documentation.

Weapons

Knives, bows, rifles, ammo purchases, and weapon-wheel use have been validated, with a known bow reload issue.

Hunting

Some animals can be skinned successfully. Several carcass/model cases remain under investigation.
